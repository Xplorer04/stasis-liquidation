## II. Weekly Audit: Pattern Recognition

At the end of the week, review your logs. Look for the **Code in the Noise.**

### 1. Identify the Loop
Do you see the same Narrative appearing multiple times?
* *Example:* If "I'm not ready yet" appears 5 times, you are in a **Perfectionism Loop**.
* *Example:* If "They are wrong" appears 5 times, you are in a **Control Loop**.

### 2. Measure the Debt
How much **Temporal Energy** did these loops consume this week?
* *Estimate:* (e.g., "I spent 4 hours looping on anxiety about the email.")

### 3. The Intermittent Great Effort
Select the **Single Loudest Loop** from this week.
* **The Action:** Schedule one specific **Intermittent Great Effort** to break it.
* *Example:* If the loop is "Avoiding the Dentist," the Action is "Call the Dentist."
* *Example:* If the loop is "Resentment at Work," the Action is "Have the difficult conversation."
